Violent Video Games Blog Posts
Go to Blog Posts

I wrote a blog post examining the current psychological research on violent video games, followed by a post where I analyzed existing datasets using R.

Background

Being an avid gamer, I was interested in looking at the existing psychological research on violent video games and any potential deleterious effects they might have on people who play them.

Research

For the first post, I gathered and read a large amount of psychology research papers from Google Scholar. For the analysis follow-up post, I learned how to scour data tables from various websites using R and then used my existing R skills to organize and statistically analyze them.

Communication

My intention was to write in such a way that a high-school educated, curious layperson could enjoy my posts. To do so, I broke the posts up with sections, small paragraphs, and illustrations, to make them more engaging.

For the statistical analysis portion I also tried to write clean, simple code with lots of comments to make it more understandable.

Overall, it was a challenge to synthesize a vast amount of technical research and summarize it in a clear way without oversimplifying it. To make sure the posts were understandable, I had my friend, who works as an editor at a newspaper, critique and edit them.
